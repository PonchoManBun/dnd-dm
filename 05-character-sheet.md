# 05 — Character Sheet

## Ability Scores

The six core D&D abilities, each with a score (3–20+) and derived modifier:

| Ability | Governs |
|---------|---------|
| **STR** | Melee attack/damage, carry capacity, athletics |
| **DEX** | AC, ranged attack, initiative, stealth, acrobatics |
| **CON** | Hit points, concentration saves |
| **INT** | Arcana, history, investigation, wizard spellcasting |
| **WIS** | Perception, insight, survival, cleric/ranger spellcasting |
| **CHA** | Persuasion, deception, intimidation, paladin spellcasting |

## Derived Stats

- **Hit Points** — Hit die per level + CON modifier
- **Armor Class** — Base 10 + DEX mod + armor/shield
- **Initiative** — DEX modifier (+ bonuses)
- **Proficiency Bonus** — +2 at level 1, scales per SRD
- **Saving Throws** — Two proficient saves per class
- **Passive Perception** — 10 + WIS mod + proficiency (if proficient)

## Skills

Full D&D 5e skill list (18 skills). Proficiency determined at character creation. Expertise available to Rogues.

## Encumbrance

Weight-based: carrying capacity = STR × 15 lbs. Exceeding capacity imposes movement and combat penalties per SRD rules.

## Spellcasting

Spell slots follow Vancian magic (SRD tables). Spellbook is an inventory item — lose it and you lose prepared spell access.
